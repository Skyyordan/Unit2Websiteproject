About Me

I was born in San Diego but raised in Los Angeles and New York. I have two brothers Nico and G, my dad is Puerto Rican and my mom is Mexican and Puerto Rican. I have a dog named luna, but if I had to switch her out for another animal it would be snake.

I love to travel, and my favorite trips have been to Iceland and Spain. I especially loved Madrid and Ibiza.

I enjoy listening to Rap, Spanish music, and EDM. One of my favorite things is going to Coachella and doing the camping experience.

I love fashion! If I won the lottery, I would definitely go on a huge shopping spree on Depop!

I love trying new foods, especially seafood and soul food. One of my favorite foods right now is seafood, and I also love the buffalo cauliflower from Erewhon.
